Admissions FAQs

What are the admission requirements?
To be considered for admission to our school, applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent, and must meet certain GPA and test score requirements. Please see our website for more information on specific requirements.
Can I apply online?
Yes, you can submit your application online through our website. Simply follow the instructions on the admissions page to complete the application process.
Is financial aid available?
Yes, financial aid is available to qualified students. Please visit our financial aid page for more information on how to apply for financial aid and the types of aid available.
